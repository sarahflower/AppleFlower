About Me

Hi! My name is Apple Flower. I'm about 5 years young and live in Roseville, California. I was adopted from the Placer County Society for Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (SPCA). I'm probably a Staffordshire Terrier / Boxer mix ... but I'm really just a mutt! I live with my two humans, my brother Teddy, and two cats that I am certain are here to steal our souls and/or treats.

If you've been looking closely at my pictures, you may have guessed that my favorite place in the world is on the couch. I am really good at stealing people's spots if they get up from the couch for even a couple of seconds. You can also often find me swaddled in a blanket, because I'm always cold! I promise the sweaters I wear aren't just because I look cute in them (but let's be real, I do), they're also functional.

If you're thinking about bringing home a new furry friend for your family, please consider adopting from an organization like the SPCA. There are a lot of animals out there that need loving homes. Adopting a shelter pet means saving a life! ♥
